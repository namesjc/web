Walking towards an uncertain future

It is an ordinary morning, you are going through your many emails, and a message pops up announcing a reorganization of your unit. The agile transformation is here and you cannot help but feel skeptical and insecure. Many questions are popping up; “What will happen with me?”, “How is this going to work exactly?”, “What if I don’t manage the new set up?” Diana Mangold went through this exact process during the Power Tools Home and Garden agile transformation and in her spark, she shares what this significant change felt like for her and what its outcomes have been. Watch Diana Mangold’s spark to better understand what agile transformation entails.
